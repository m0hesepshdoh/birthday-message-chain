    What is the Birthday Message Chain?

    The Birthday Message Chain is a community project where people share birthday wishes. When you submit a message, you'll receive a random birthday message from someone else on your special day!

    How does it work?

    1. Submit your birthday and a heartfelt message
    2. Your message gets added to our database
    3. On your birthday, we'll email you a random message from another participant
    4. Your message will be shared with someone else on their birthday

    Is my personal information safe?

    We take privacy seriously. We only collect your email and birthday (month/day, not year). Your email will only be used to send you a birthday message and will never be shared publicly or with third parties.

    Can I submit multiple messages?

    To keep it fair for everyone, we limit submissions to one message per email address. This helps ensure everyone gets a unique message on their birthday.

    What should I write in my message?

    Write something kind, uplifting, and birthday-appropriate! Some ideas:
    - May your birthday be as wonderful as you are!
    - Wishing you a year filled with joy and success!
    - Hope your special day is as amazing as you deserve!
    Keep it positive and under 100 characters.

    Can I delete my message later?

    Yes! If you'd like to remove your message from the chain, simply email us at ug671431015@ftu.ac.th from the address you used to submit and we'll remove your entry.
