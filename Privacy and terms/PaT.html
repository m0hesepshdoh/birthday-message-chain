Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Birthday Chain ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use our service:

  • Email Address: Used to send you a birthday message and for service communications
  • Birthday Information: Month and day (no year collected) to match messages
  • Birthday Message: The content you submit to share with others
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and device information for analytics

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our service
  • To send you a random birthday message on your birthday
  • To share your message (anonymously) with another user on their birthday
  • To improve and optimize our service
  • To prevent abuse and enforce our terms

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. Your data may be shared in these limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third parties who assist in operating our service (e.g., email delivery, hosting)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Anonymous Data: Aggregated, anonymized data may be used for analytics

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies and similar technologies:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for basic functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with our service
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your language preference

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Data encryption in transit (SSL/TLS)
  • Secure storage with access controls
  • Regular security reviews

While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.
